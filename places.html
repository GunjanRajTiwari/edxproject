Explore Nepal

Everest Tallest Mountain of the world located in Solukhumbu District. Its height is 8848 m above sea level.
Chitwan Land famous for forest and wildlife and mainly known for CHitwan National Park where one horn Rhino is found.
Lumbini Birth place of the light of Asia, Gautam Buddha. This place is full of stupas.
Kathmandu Capital of Nepal and place full of temples and historical Durbars..
Rara Biggest and most beautiful lake of Nepal and destination for trekking.
Pokhara Tourist capitals of Nepal. Full of beautiful lakes, caves and beautiful scenery of the Himalayas.
